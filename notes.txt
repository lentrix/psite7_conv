Election Flow
- Preparatory State
	Admins will select and indicate all the participants who are
	eligible to become an officer.
	Deliverable: List of members eligible for officer
- Nomination State
	Nomination page for all active members will be activated
	and will accept nominations
- On-Going State
	Election Forms will be available on designated
	devices where participants are able to vote up
	to a designated number.
	If the participant has already voted, the election
	page will display to whom the participant has voted for.
- Closed State
	Election page will show the result of the election.
